Thank you for visiting this web site.

This site is the final project of the Full-Stack Developer training I attended in the 2nd quarter of 2021. While I do have a significant programming experience from the past, I had not been coding professionally for years. Then I decided it was time to really get current with the languages, tools and frameworks in use today.
I took the opportunity of this project to develop something that will be immediately useful to me and that I have been missing so far. Essentially, I wish to regain control over my digital self and stop letting my personal data be stored I do know where and be exploited I do not know how by I do not know who. So, My Own Private Cloud is my first serious effort to act upon this aspiration.
This project is using most of the topics covered during the training: HTML, CSS (incl. Flexbox, Grid, Bootstrap), Javascript, Python, SQL, Django, GitHub. Plus some more: Flask, Heroku, Linux, Apache. To be in control of one's data, it is little surprising that one needs a fairly good vertical understanding of all the layers involved. But no matter how complex, I aim at packaging an actionable solution that not only I but also other motivated people can actually use on a daily basis.
